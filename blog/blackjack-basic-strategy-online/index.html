The Power of Basic Strategy

Blackjack is unique among casino games because a player's decisions directly influence the house edge. If a player approaches the table without a plan, the house retains a significant advantage. However, by applying "basic strategy"—a mathematically proven set of rules that dictates the best move for every possible hand combination—the house edge can be slashed to as low as 0.05%.

Basic strategy takes the guesswork out of the game. It tells you exactly when to hit, stand, double down, or split based on your cards and the dealer’s up-card. While professional players may utilize card counting to track the deck’s composition, the average online player will see the most consistent improvement in their results by mastering these foundational, optimal decisions.
