The German Regulatory Landscape in 2026

The German gambling market operates under the Interstate Treaty on Gambling, which imposes clear boundaries to ensure player protection. Unlike markets where regulation is still fragmented, German-facing operators must adhere to strict licensing criteria that emphasize social responsibility and fraud prevention. For players, this means that most platforms targeting the region will display clear licensing badges, often from the GGL (Gemeinsame Glücksspielbehörde der Länder).
